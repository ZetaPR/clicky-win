Active Feature: Multi-Step Guidance

Spec: docs/superpowers/specs/2026-05-11-multi-step-design.md

Section 1 — Architecture (Approved)

  • StepPlanStore (Clicky.Services) — holds plan in memory: step index, original screenshot, conversation history, target HWND. Clears on end/timeout.
  • StepClickWatcher (Clicky.Services) — arms after step delivered, subscribes to SharpHook RawMousePressed, triggers verify on click within ±60px of target in right window.
  • StepVerifier (Clicky.Services) — waits 300ms post-click, grabs fresh screenshot, sends to worker with history. Worker returns: advance | correct | complete.
  • ForegroundGuard — ~5 lines in existing 60fps OnMainTick. Fires drift/timeout state machine when user leaves target app.
  • Overlay — new OverlayState.WaitingForStep (triangle stays pinned + pulses). New "Step N of M" badge with bounding-box collision avoidance.
  • CompanionOrchestrator — branches on result type after StreamLlmToTtsAsync instead of always calling ReturnToIdle.
  • Worker — two new modes: mode:"plan" and mode:"verify". History is client-side in StepPlanStore.

Section 2 — Step Plan Format (Approved)

  • Lazy coordinates — only step 1 gets coordinates in the initial plan. Steps 2+ get coordinates from the verify response (fresh post-action screenshot).
  • Tag format — [STEP:1:x,y:label]text[/STEP] for step 1; [STEP:2]text[/STEP] for subsequent steps.
  • Incremental parse — PendingStep accumulates text until [/STEP], materialises into a real Step. Step 1 TTS starts immediately — no waiting for full plan.
  • Verify double-duty — verify response returns {result, nextX, nextY, nextLabel}.

Section 3 — Behaviour Decisions (Approved)

  • Timeout: 30 seconds of no click → StepClickWatcher disarms, overlay idles, StepPlanStore clears.
  • PTT during sequence: cancels entirely. No resume. Pipeline starts fresh.
  • Trigger: Claude decides — no client-side detection. Worker sends every request in the same format; Claude returns [POINT:...] or [STEP:...] based on judgement.

Design complete. Next: implementation plan.
